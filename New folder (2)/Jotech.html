Brief Information about St Joseph Technical Institue Kwahu Tafo
St. Joseph's Technical Institute, Kwahu is a co-educational mixed Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) located at KwahuTafo in Eastern Region within Kwahu South district which was established in 1982.

Admissions

New students are admitted every September through a computerized system managed by the Ghana Education Service.
Admission is based on the Basic Education Certificate Examination (B.E.C.E) results, held annually for junior high school students at various centers nationwide.
