St. Joseph's Technical Institute, Kwahu is a co-educational mixed Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET)
located at KwahuTafo in Eastern Region within Kwahu South district which was established in 1982.

St Joseph Technical Institue has stood for so many years and
still improving.

St. Joseph Tech. institution was established by the Missionaries of the Catholic Diocese in Tamale in 1967.
It eventually turn into a government assisted school 1984 and gained boarding status in the year 2002.
The school is situated about hundred meters behind the Roman Catholic Parish.
St. Joseph Technical Institute is one of the best Technical Institutes in Ghana.
